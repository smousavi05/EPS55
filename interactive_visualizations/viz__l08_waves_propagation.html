Understanding Seismic Wave Complexity

Refraction

Waves bend when entering layers with different velocities, following Snell's Law

Reflection

Part of the wave energy reflects at layer boundaries, creating multiple arrivals

Mode Conversion

P-waves can convert to S-waves (and vice versa) at interfaces

Critical Angles

At certain angles, waves travel along layer boundaries as head waves
